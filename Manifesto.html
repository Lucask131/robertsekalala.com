LEADERSHIP WITH INTEGRITY

People First: A New Dawn of Inclusive Leadership

My fellow Ugandans, today, I stand before you not as a politician, but as a servant of the people — as your brother, your neighbor, your voice. My agenda is built around real needs, practical solutions, and a strong vision for transformation. I have walked the potholed roads of Trading centers. I have listened to the cries of mothers in clinics without medicine, of youth without jobs, of children learning without enough resources and I say this: we can do better and we must do God willing plus our effort.





I believe in a Uganda where every citizen young or old, rich or poor has the opportunity to live a healthy, educated, and empowered life. I come to you with a plan, practical, focused to improve on some of what we already have. We have hospitals — but many lack medicine and equipment. We have schools — but some still lack teachers and materials. We have youth — skilled, brilliant, ready — but locked out of opportunity. Together, we can fix this. Together, we can rise.

HEALTH FOR EVERYONE

When our people are healthy, our nation is strong. Because good health is not a privilege but rather a right then every member of the community is entitled to the following services when given our power:

  • No mother should ever die while giving life.
  • No child should grow up without access to a doctor.
  • Under my leadership, we shall rebuild our health system from the ground up.
  • Recruit and train more health workers, especially in underserved areas.
  • Free maternal care for every expectant mother.
  • Upgrade and equip rural health centers with modern facilities.
  • Expand vaccination programs and community health outreach.
  • Supply every health center with essential drugs and equipment.
  • Ensure regular supervision and accountability in public health units.
  • Increase funding for community health outreaches.
  • Improve pay and working conditions for health workers.
  • Introduce digital health systems for better patient tracking and transparency.

EDUCATION THAT EMPOWERS

Education is the most powerful weapon we can use to change Uganda. We will build a future where every child can dream — and achieve. I pledge:

  • Modern learning materials and digital tools in classrooms.
  • Better pay and training for our hardworking teachers to improve learning quality.
  • School feeding programs to support children from humble background communities.
  • Construction of more schools in remote areas.
  • Train and recruit more teachers, especially in rural areas.
  • Supply books, computers, and learning materials to existing schools.
  • Repair old school buildings and build sanitation facilities.
  • Introduce solar power to schools that lack electricity.
  • Expand school lunch programs to keep children in class.

Let’s give our children the tools to succeed in the schools that already exist.

YOUTH: THE PILLARS OF OUR NATION

Uganda is a young nation — not just in age, but in spirit. Our youth are innovative, bold, and ready to build. What they lack is opportunity — and I’m here to open those doors. I commit to:

  • Startup interest-free loans and grants for youth-led businesses.
  • Vocational training centers across regions.
  • Jobs, internships, and ICT hubs in our community.
  • A National Youth Fund to turn ideas into impact.
  • Startup capital and grants for youth-led businesses.
  • Vocational training and ICT centers in our community.
  • Youth internship and job placement programs.
  • National Youth Fund to support innovation and entrepreneurship.
  • Promote sports, arts, and talent development.
  • Create local internships linked with existing government offices and businesses.
  • Upgrade existing youth centers into innovation and job training hubs.
  • Offer small grants for youth groups with business ideas.
  • Partner with private companies to provide digital skills training.
  • Launch a Youth Employment Portal that connects youth to local jobs and gigs.

With the above fulfilled to every young person: you are not the problem — you are the solution.

FINAL WORD

Fellow Ugandans, this election is not just about politics. It is about direction. Which will not continue with the same system that leaves many behind but rather improves what we already have and make it work for every citizen. I am not here to make promises I cannot keep. I am here to work with you to restore faith in what is already ours, and make it better. Let us rise together for a better, stronger Uganda. Vote Robert Ssekalala. Vote for improvement, not illusion.
